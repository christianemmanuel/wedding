Travel by Public Transport

From SM San Jose del Monte (tungko)

  • Ride a Bus going to Sapang Palay, preferably Santrans, then baba ng Sampol.
  • Ride a Jeep going to Sta. Maria then ask kuyang driver to drop you off to The Grounds.

From SM North

  • Find an FX enroute to Tungko, then alight to SM San Jose del Monte (tungko).
  • Ride a Bus going to Sapang Palay, preferably Santrans, then baba ng Sampol.
  • Ride a Jeep going to Sta. Maria then ask kuyang driver to drop you off to The Grounds.

From SM Megamall

  • Ride an MRT from Shaw or Ortigas Station going to Quezon Ave.
  • Ride a Bus going to Sapang Palay, then baba ng Sampol.
  • Ride a Jeep going to Sta. Maria then ask kuyang driver to drop you off to The Grounds.
